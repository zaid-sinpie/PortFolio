1 HYDROPONIC

Hydrophonic water level and humidity monitoring and controlling system

* Components Used *

ESP32 DHT22 OLED DISPLAY RELAY WATER PUMP LED BMS BREADBOARD PCB
SOFTWARE

* I have used ESP32 as my main microcontroller to sense the input and control the output.

* I have selected ESP32 because it has inbuilt bluetooth and wifi modules init.

* I have used DHT22 sensore to sense both the humidity and temperature of the hydroponic system.

* I have displayed the temperature and humidity over a OLED display to monitor them continuesly.

* Also connected this system with the thingspeak cloud platform to check the temperature and humidity over smart devices.

* The water pump powered by relay turns on whenever the humidity goes below 40% and after some delay it will turn off automatically.

* The LED's turns on only for one hour in a day for everyday.
